# Privacy Policy

**intro-skipper - Last updated: July 1, 2026**

## Overview

This document describes how the JellyWatch Wear OS application collects, handles, and processes data. JellyWatch is a standalone Jellyfin client for watches. The app is designed to connect only to the Jellyfin server you choose and does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, or tracking mechanisms.

## App Privacy Policy

Applies to: **JellyWatch** - published on Google Play.

### Data Collection

JellyWatch does not collect personal information for intro-skipper or any developer-operated service. The app handles only the information needed to connect to and use your Jellyfin server:

- **Server URL**: the address of the Jellyfin server you enter or select.
- **Username and password**: used only when you choose manual sign-in. The password is sent directly to your Jellyfin server for authentication and is not stored by JellyWatch.
- **Quick Connect code and secret**: used temporarily when you sign in with Jellyfin Quick Connect.
- **Jellyfin access token**: issued by your Jellyfin server after successful sign-in and used for subsequent Jellyfin requests.
- **Jellyfin user ID and display name**: used to request your libraries, playback state, and account-specific media lists.
- **Device ID**: a random identifier generated by JellyWatch and sent to Jellyfin as part of the client authentication header.
- **App preferences**: local settings such as which home screen sections are enabled.

No analytics, crash-reporting SDKs, advertising identifiers, or other tracking mechanisms are present in the app.

### Data Storage

JellyWatch stores the server URL, Jellyfin access token, Jellyfin user ID, display name, generated device ID, and app preferences locally in the app's private Android SharedPreferences storage.

The app does not store your Jellyfin password. The app also sets Android backup to disabled, so app data is not intentionally included in Android cloud backup by JellyWatch.

You may delete all locally stored JellyWatch data at any time by clearing the app's data in Android Settings or by uninstalling the app.

### Data Transmitted

JellyWatch communicates with the Jellyfin server address that you provide or select. Depending on how you use the app, JellyWatch may send requests to your Jellyfin server for:

- Authentication and Quick Connect approval.
- Library, media item, image, and search data.
- Direct playback and transcoded playback streams.
- Playback progress and playback state updates.
- Media segment data used for skipping intros, credits, commercials, or similar segments.

JellyWatch does not send your credentials, token, media library details, playback activity, or app preferences to intro-skipper servers or to third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services.

Content displayed by the app, including artwork and video, is fetched from your Jellyfin server. Artwork may be cached in memory while the app is running.

### Data Controller

intro-skipper is the data controller for any data processed by this app as publisher of JellyWatch. Contact us at [info@intro-skipper.org](mailto:info@intro-skipper.org).

Your Jellyfin server is operated by you or a third party of your choosing. Data processed by that server is subject to the privacy practices, retention settings, and access controls of that Jellyfin server.

### Your Rights

Even though JellyWatch does not collect personal data for intro-skipper, you retain all rights granted by applicable privacy law, including GDPR rights where applicable. To exercise any right or ask a privacy question, contact us at the address above or open an issue in the project repository.

## Third-Party Services

### Google Play

JellyWatch is distributed through Google Play. Google may collect device, install, diagnostics, and usage information under Google's own privacy policy: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Your Jellyfin Server

All Jellyfin account, media, image, playback, and media segment data is read from or written to the Jellyfin server you configure. JellyWatch does not control that server's privacy practices.

## Google Play Data Safety Notes

For Google Play Data safety form purposes, JellyWatch itself does not collect or share user data with the developer or third-party services. The app does transmit user-provided credentials and media-related requests to the user's own Jellyfin server as necessary for app functionality.

JellyWatch requests these Android permissions:

- `INTERNET`: required to connect to your Jellyfin server.
- `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE`: used to understand network availability.
- `WAKE_LOCK`: used to keep playback behavior reliable on Wear OS.

## Children

JellyWatch is not directed to children. The app connects to user-provided Jellyfin servers and does not provide a developer-operated content service.

## Security

Use HTTPS when connecting to Jellyfin over untrusted networks. JellyWatch does not disable certificate validation for HTTPS connections. Plain HTTP may be used on trusted local networks if you choose to connect that way.
